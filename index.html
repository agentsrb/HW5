Scrabble! Drag and drop your tiles from the rack onto the desired board space. All tiles after the first must be placed directly adjacent to an existing tile on either the right or left. Valid placements will be highlighted in green. Invalid placements will be highlighted in red. Once you have completed your desired word click 'Submit Word' to calculate the score. 'Submit Word' and 'Clear Board' will delete any active tiles on the game board from the bag. You can reset the tiles avaliable in the rack by pressing 'Swap Hand', doing so will return the tiles from your rack to the bag. When you have finished playing you can reset the game to it's default state by pressing 'Restart Game'.

The current distribution of tiles in your bag is displayed below the rack.
